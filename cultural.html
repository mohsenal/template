Executive Summary

Ghetto Culture is spreading around the world and there are negative consequences on today’s youth. The aim of this proposal is to describe what exactly ghetto culture is, why it’s important to reduce and reverse its expansion, and what role the individual plays in this process. WorldStarHipHop is a website that demonstrates the effects of embracing such a mentality by promoting videos of people immersed in ghetto culture, and it is increasing in popularity. This structure of this proposal begins with an introduction of the concept and the issues at hand, a description of WorldStarHipHop.com and its implications, a baseline definition of ghetto culture and why its expansion should be challenged, and finally the proposal is concluded with a set of solutions and their anticipated aftereffects.
