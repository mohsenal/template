Introduction: The Glorification of Ghetto Culture, and the Role of WorldStarHipHop

There is a social phenomenon spreading worldwide that not everyone is aware of, with dangerous consequences. This phenomenon is of course the glorification of ghetto culture, spreading at a disturbing rate in today’s society. Journalist and activist Cora Daniels describes this anomaly as “Ghettonation”, a mindset that she says “celebrates the worst of human nature…has taken hold coast to coast” (MacDonald). This ghetto mentality is exemplified in WorldStarHipHop.com, a website that posts content that reflects many ideologies of the ghetto. Ghetto culture is spreading, and the increasing popularity of WorldStarHipHop.com is a testament to that fact.
