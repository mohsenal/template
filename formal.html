Executive Summary

The purpose of this report is to provide an overview of the academic discipline of health care and to discuss medical research within that discipline and in the medical field in general. Research is the driving force behind the field of medicine. Every aspect of the medical field, from standards to procedures, treatments to equipment was heavily researched and reviewed. New techniques, medicines, and ground breaking discoveries are the result of research in the medical field. In the field of medicine, the results of various forms of research are presented to establish the results of said research in the field. This allows for the implementation of the information, further research, and necessary revision. Communication occurs in the medical field through research. The flow of information needs to be reliable and efficient. In presenting medical research, the conductors of the study are communicating what they have found to the medical community.
